ABOUT THE SITE

This organization was established with the sole objective of helping the families of soldiers who lost their lives fighting bravely for our better tomorrows.

The Trust provides for the education of martyr's kids, the family's health and for other basic necessities. During the major festivals, the trust also sends out gifts to the families as a symbol of love. What we've done for people

Our main objective is to To help relocate and find housing for our soldiers in need.

To develop and provide hostel facilities for children and orphans of ex servicemen, widows and serving soldiers.

To provide financial assistance to wards of ex-servicemen who are destitute/disabled/handicapped on case to case basis.
